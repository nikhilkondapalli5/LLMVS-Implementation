The video, produced by The Argentina Independent, explores the culture of dog grooming in Buenos Aires, a city where the desire to be presentable extends beyond people to their pets. The feature centers on Sebastian's, a dog beauty parlor in the Recoleta neighborhood, where dogs of all sizes come for a "model makeover." The chief groomer, Hugo, is highlighted for his comprehensive services, which include haircuts, baths, hair drying, nail trimming, and ear cleaning, and for his ability to handle approximately 25 dogs a day. Owners attest to Hugo's skill, reporting that their dogs, even those with strong personalities, become calm and quiet in Hugo's hands. Hugo shares an anecdote illustrating his expertise, detailing how he was able to clean the ears of a German Shepherd that required three people to manage at home, and he notes that many animals are so comfortable that they know they are coming and will jump right into the bathtub. Ultimately, the video suggests that for these fashionable canines, "when you look good, you feel good.
