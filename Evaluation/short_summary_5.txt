This GMC Certified Service video highlights the importance of tire maintenance, emphasizing that their experts are available to assist when it is time for replacements. The narrator explains that although all tires wear out, rotating them helps extend their lifespan and ensures even wear, with a specific recommendation to rotate tires every 7,500 miles. Additionally, the video stresses the need for proper tire balance, warning that unbalanced wheels can cause the vehicle to vibrate and create uneven tread wear. The segment concludes by showing a customer interacting with a service representative, reinforcing the support provided by certified professionals.
